Starsim Explained

What is Starsim?

Starsim is a cutting-edge agent-based disease modeling framework that allows users to simulate pathogen progression within dynamic networks.

Why Starsim Matters?

Starsim is crucial for understanding disease spread dynamics and testing intervention strategies in a virtual environment.

How to Use Starsim?

Users can easily design and configure simulations to study pathogen transmission and agent interactions in a realistic setting.

Is Starsim Free?

Yes, Starsim is an open-source software provided by our non-profit organization for the scientific community.

Can Starsim Predict Outbreaks?

While Starsim provides valuable insights, it's not a crystal ball; it helps in understanding disease dynamics and potential scenarios.
